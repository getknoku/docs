# Widget

The Knoku widget is an embeddable AI chat panel for documentation sites. Visitors ask questions and get answers with citations linking back to your docs.

The widget renders inside a Shadow DOM, so your site's CSS doesn't leak in and Knoku's styles don't leak out. Dark mode follows your site automatically.

## Get started

1. **[Install](/widget/installation)**: add the script tag (or npm package) and verify it mounts.
2. **Pick a setup**: start from a real scenario below, copy the snippet, adjust.
3. **Reference**: when you need the full attribute list or the runtime API.

## Common setups

Each setup is one focused page with a working example. Most sites combine two or three.

- **[Add an AI button to your header](/widget/setups/custom-trigger)**: open chat from your own button, search bar, or a keyboard shortcut. Hide the default launcher when you want a single branded entry.
- **[Match your brand](/widget/setups/branding)**: set your accent color, override panel header, suggestion chips, hover states, and dark variants.
- **[Choose a panel layout](/widget/setups/panel-layout)**: panel floats above your content (default) or pushes the page over while open — pick what fits your site.
- **[Require consent before chat](/widget/setups/consent)**: show a consent screen before the first question; held questions resume after acceptance.
- **[Translate the widget](/widget/setups/localize)**: pick from 13 built-in languages or auto-detect from the browser; provide your own greeting and suggested questions in any language.
- **[Identify logged-in users](/widget/setups/identify-users)**: associate chat sessions with known users for auth-gated docs and richer analytics.
